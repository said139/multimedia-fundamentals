Question 3 — 4 Points

Video Bitrate

Uncompressed bitrate for a 720p HD video stream

Problem Statement

A video is 1280 × 720, 30 fps, and 24 bits per pixel. Estimate its uncompressed bitrate.

Key Concepts
  • Bitrate — number of bits processed per second (bps).
  • Frame rate (fps) — how many full image frames are displayed each second.
  • Uncompressed — no codec applied; every pixel at full colour depth every frame.
  • 1 Mbps = 1,000,000 bps  |  1 Gbps = 1,000 Mbps
Step-by-Step Solution

Pixels per frame: 1280 × 720 = 921,600 pixels

Bits per frame: 921,600 × 24 = 22,118,400 bits

Bits per second: 22,118,400 × 30 = 663,552,000 bps

Convert to Mbps: 663,552,000 ÷ 1,000,000 ≈ 663.55 Mbps

Bitrate = 1280 × 720 × 30 × 24 = 663,552,000 bps ≈ 663.55 Mbps
✓ Uncompressed Bitrate ≈ 663.55 Mbps
